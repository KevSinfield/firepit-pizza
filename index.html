Our story

Born from a love of fire and dough

Placeholder — your friend's real story goes here

Firepit started with a simple obsession: pizza cooked properly, over real wood flame. After years of tinkering with dough recipes at home and feeding friends from a tiny garden oven, we built a mobile pizzeria to bring that same fire-blistered pizza to the streets.

Every dough is slow-proved for 48 hours, topped with the best ingredients we can find, and cooked in front of you in 90 seconds. No shortcuts, no fuss — just proper pizza and a bit of fire-side theatre.
